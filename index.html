ABOUT US

History

Silk Fitness Stores is a fitness equipment store that is dedicated to providing quality fitness equipment. Founded in 2013 by fitness enthusiasts Shane Williams and Amir Kapoor, Silk Fitness Stores was conceived as the answer to affordable and quality personal fitness accessories and equipment. With four offices and over 500 sales points natiowide, Silk Fitness Stores is now a well-established fitness equipment brand with customers across the United States and around the world.



Why use us

With Silk Fitness Stores your search for affordable and quality personal fitness accessories is finally over. Each item sold by Silk Fitness Stores is specially made and quality-assured for premium customer satisfaction. We understand that our customers desire quality fitness gear that would last a long time. Every item on our line has been tested and approved. Even our fitness-buff founders cannot get enough of theirs! 

Our business is also sustainable. We are an eco-friendly business, and the United Nations SDGs remain very important to us as we serve our customers. We maintain an environmentally friendly work culture:

  • Energy efficient power in all our offices. We shut down all appliances and close work on time daily.
  • Our staff are encouraged to cycle to work at least once a week.
  • 5% of all sales profit goes to the Global Foundation for Climate Change.
