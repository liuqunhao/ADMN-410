Policies

Finance Policy

  1. Customer Authority for necessary and regular financial actions and decisions is the responsibility of the Silk Fitness Stores management team.
  2. Silk Fitness Stores maintains a policy statement on conflicts of interest or insider transactions.
  3. Silk Fitness Stores has appointed a Head of Finance with clear authority to spend funds, including approval, check signing, and payroll.
  4. Silk Fitness Stores has appointed a legal team with the clear assignment of authority to enter into contracts.
  5. Silk Fitness Stores has appointed an accountant with the clear responsibility for maintaining accurate financial records.


Delivery Policy

  1. Shipment processing time: 14 business days.
  2. Shipping rates & delivery estimates: Will be stated on the quote provided to the customer.
  3. Shipment to P.O. boxes: Will be fulfilled according to the information provided by the customer.
  4. Shipment confirmation and order tracking: Information will be provided to the customer in real time.
  5. Customs, duties and taxes: Information will be provided to the customer before the order is confirmed.


Return Policy

  1. Customers have up to 30 days to request a refund.
  2. A Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) must be requested.
  3. The serial number of the affected product must be verified first.
  4. Silk Fitness Stores will pay for any shipping involved (with certain exceptions).
  5. Refunds are credited back to the customer's credit/debit cards by Silk Fitness Stores.
